About Us

We believe in a balanced and balanced body. We offer a wide range of personal training, and we can help you achieve these goals. The studio comes with light weight machines, weighted balls, and free weights. Our classes are for ALL levels of fitness. If you are looking to workout with a group of like minded individuals, or you are looking to improve your strength and flexibility, this is the studio for you. Our instructors are highly trained and have a passion for fitness and health. We are located in the beautiful town of Killeen, Texas. You can find us at the Killeen Farmers Market. Welcome all fitness levels and can tailor a session to fit your needs.We believe that exercise does more than just look good!We believe when you feel good you will be a better you, and we offer a variety of classes to suit every need. Come check us out today! We are locally owned and operated and offer a friendly and knowledgeable environment, a cozy, modern studio with a friendly staff, and a personal approach to help you take your health and fitness to the next level.Our students come from a wide variety of ethnic backgrounds.We believe that exercise is the key to overall health, and hope we can make a positive impact in your life by making you feel good. Body is our Place! Dont you just want to be your best self? We create an environment that transforms the body into a place of clarity, balance, and freedom. We offer a variety of classes to suit your needs, from yoga, to Zumba, Pilates, and MELT classes.We believe class is the most important part of a good workout! At this studio, we believe we are a part of a community. Our goal is to inspire and motivate you to push your limits, become the best you can be, and keep pushing. We are a multi-disciplinary team of professionals with a common goal - to improve the fitness and wellness of each individual through the use of innovative and effective fitness techniques and coaching. Our goal is to help you maximize your health and wellness!
